1 Introduction

This script fully documents the results and control analyses reported in

Montero-Melis et al., 2021. No evidence for embodiment: The motor system is not needed to keep action words in working memory. Submitted to Cortex (Stage 2 Registered Report).

The script follows our pre-registered analyses as specified in the Stage 1 submission of our Registered Report, which can be found here. The analyses are ordered in the same way as in the article.

4 Main results with \(N=77\) (all participants)

We continued collecting participants while processing the data for our first pre-registered analysis for \(N=60\). We stopped data collection when the analysis with \(N=60\) showed reliable evidence in favor of the null hypothesis (based on our pre-defined Bayes factor, see manuscript and below). By then we had collected 77 participants in total. Here we report the results of the analyses with all 77 participants, which constitute our main results.
